Beef Stew

  • Prep Time: 10 Minutes
  • Cook Time: 150 Minutes
  • Serves: 6

** Recipe can be doubled or tripled if needed **

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • 1 Tbsps
  • 2 Pounds Stew Meat
  • 1 Medium Onion, Diced
  • 3 Garlic cloves , Minced
  • 1 can of Beer(12 Oz. Size)
  • 4 cups Beef Stock
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • 1/2 tsp Paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Kosher Salt
  • Freshly Ground Black Pepper
  • 1-1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 4 Carrots, Washed, Unpeeled, And Roughly Sliced
  • 4 New Potatoes, Quartered
  • Minced Parsley (optional)

Step 1: Heat oil and butter in a large pot over medium-high heat. Brown meat in two batches, setting aside on a plate when brown. Cut pieces in half. Set aside.

Step 2:Add diced onions to the pot. Stir and cook for two or three minutes until softened, then add garlic for another minute. Pour in beer and beef stock, then add Worcestershire, tomato paste, paprika, salt, pepper, and sugar. Add beef back into the pot. Stir to combine. Cover and simmer for 1 1/2 to 2 hours.

Step 3:*UPDATE: The liquid should cook down to a thicker state. If it gets too thick/reduces too much, add additional water as needed.

Step 4:Add carrots and potatoes, then cover and cook for an additional 30 minutes. (If stew gets dry, just add a cup of hot water at a time to replenish the liquid.) Taste and adjust seasonings as needed.

Step 5:Serve in bowls next to crusty French bread. Sprinkle with minced parsley, if desired.

Pin Wheels

  • Prep Time: 15 Minutes
  • Ready in: 45 Minutes
  • Serves: 6

** Recipe can be doubled or tripled if needed **

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 Ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (1 Ounce) package ranch salad dressing mix
  • 3 greens onions, chopped
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 11 (8 inch) flour tortillas
  • 22 slices thinly sliced ham

Step 1: Beat cream cheese, avocado, and salad dressing mix in a small bowl; stir in onions and bell peppers. Spread 3 tablespoons cream cheese mixture over each tortilla; top each tortilla with two slices ham.

Step 2:Roll tortillas up tightly and wrap in plastic wrap; refrigerate until firm, about 30 minutes. Unwrap and cut into 3/4-inch slices.

Pin Wheels

  • Prep Time: 15 Minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 Minutes
  • Serves: 8

** Recipe can be doubled or tripled if needed **

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 package of taco seasoning
  • 1 cup shreded sharp cheedar cheese
  • 1 tube of crescent rolls

Step 1:Preheat oven to 350 degrees

Step 2:Brown ground beef, drain and return to pan.

Step 3:Add taco seasoning

Step 4:Add 2 spoonfuls of taco meat on top of each crescent roll and roll

Step 5:Cook for 15 minutes or until crecsent rolls are golden brown
